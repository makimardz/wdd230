Chamber 10th Anniversary

>

Join us for a special celebration! We are excited to announce our 10th Anniversary event and we would like to invite you to be a part of this special occasion. The event will take place on June 22nd at the Grand Hotel, located at 123 Main Street. The festivities will begin at 6 PM and will include a formal dinner, dancing, and a special program highlighting the past decade of our organization.

Dress code is black tie and tickets are available for purchase at $100 per person. We hope to see you there to celebrate this milestone with us!
