Membership Pricing

NP (Non-Profit) Membership

NP Membership includes all the benefits of a Bronze Membership, but is free for Non-Profit organizations.

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership includes an invitation to all Chamber events, a listing on the Chamber website, and a listing in the Chamber newsletter.

Silver Membership

Silver Membership includes all the benefits of a Bronze Membership, plus a 20% discount on all Chamber events.

Gold Membership

Gold Membership includes all the benefits of a Silver Membership, plus a 50% discount on all Chamber events.
